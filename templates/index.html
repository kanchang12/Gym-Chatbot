About Us

Our Story

Founded in 1995, PowerFit Equipment Co. has been at the forefront of fitness innovation for over 25 years. What started as a small family-owned business has grown into one of the most trusted names in fitness equipment manufacturing.

Our Mission

We strive to provide high-quality, innovative fitness equipment that helps people achieve their health and wellness goals. Our commitment to excellence, durability, and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the industry.

Our Values

Quality, Innovation, Integrity, and Customer Focus drive everything we do at PowerFit Equipment Co.
